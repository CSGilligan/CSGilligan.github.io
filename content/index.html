Chris Gilligan

Hi, I'm Chris Gilligan.


I'm a data journalist working on all sorts of projects, most of which will hang out here. You can find me at gilliganch@gmail.com or @gilliganchris.

As of June 2022, I'm writing full time for U.S. News & World Report, where my work can be seen on my author page. For some highlights, take a look at my pieces on the United States' lagging vaccine program and summer droughts across the country.


Check out my other work!

Outside of my work with U.S. News, my data projects can be sorted into two categories:

- On disparity in baseball - On mobility in MLB and the NBA - On spin rates and sticky stuff - On intentional walks
- On the 2022 Pennsylvania Senate primaries - On women of color mayors - On early-pandemic rent in Boston


The projects included above use a range of data cleaning, analysis, and visualization tools, including Python, HTML/CSS/JavaScript, web scraping, d3, DataWrapper, Flourish, and more.
